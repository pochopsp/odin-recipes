Spaghetti with pomodoro

Description

This is one of the most famous Italian dishes, and one you can make easily.

Ingredients

  • 500 g of Italian Spaghetti
  • 200 g of fresh tomatoes
  • 50 g of salt
  • 50 cl of olive oil
  • 20 g of garlic
  • some leaves of basil

Steps

  1. Put 2 lt of water in a pot and heat it
  2. Cut the tomatoes in little pieces
  3. Put the tomatoes in a pan, with a little bit of oil and garlic
  4. Add salt, keep squashing tomatoes until you have a cream
  5. Add some leaves of basil to the sauce
  6. Put the spaghetti in the boiling water and wait until they're ready
  7. Put the cooked spaghetti in the pan with the tomatoes, heat and serve
